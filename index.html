We create exquisite LED artwork for merrymakers to wear and gaze at. We can design, source, and build LED projects of any scale and scope, including power distribution and custom content programming.

Our headpieces are handcrafted from objects found in nature and artisinal leather, fabric and bone. They uniquely fit the aesthetic of the wearer, channel organic beauty by day and dazzle onlookers by night. They can even react to sound and motion!

Our LED installations are psychedelic and extremely bright.
